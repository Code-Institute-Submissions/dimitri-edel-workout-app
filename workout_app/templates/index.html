How to create your own list of Exercises

  1. In the navigation bar, click on Exercises
  2. Name your exercise, e.g. Push-Ups, Bicep Curl, ..
  3. Choose type
    • Strength - Exercises that involve weight lifting.
    • Cardio - Cardio vascular exercises such as jogging
  4. Choose goal
    • Repetitions - Time how many repetitions you can do within a certain period of time
    • Distance - Time how long a distance you can traverse in a certain period of time
